1. Information We Collect

Account and profile information

When you create an account, we collect information you provide, such as your email address, username, and password. You may also add profile details about yourself and your pets, including pet names, breed, age, photos, and a profile picture or avatar.

Content and social activity

We collect the content you create and share in the App, including posts, photos and videos, captions, comments, likes, follows, and direct messages exchanged with other users. We also collect information about how you interact socially, such as the accounts you follow and the content you engage with.

Location information

With your permission, we collect location information from your device to power location-based features (for example, discovering nearby pets, owners, or pet-friendly places). See Section 4 for details and how to turn this off.

Technical and log information

To operate, secure, and troubleshoot the App, we collect limited technical information such as your device type, operating system version, app version, IP address, and basic log data (for example, error and crash reports). We do not use third-party advertising trackers and we do not sell your personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and pet profiles.
  • To provide core social features — publishing posts, showing feeds, and enabling comments, likes, follows, and direct messages.
  • To enable location-based discovery when you choose to use it.
  • To personalize the content and connections you see.
  • To keep the App safe — detecting, preventing, and responding to fraud, abuse, spam, and violations of our Terms of Service.
  • To respond to your support requests and communicate with you about the App.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited situations:

  • With other users: Your username, profile, pet information, and the content you post are visible to other users according to your account and post settings. Direct messages are shared with the recipients you send them to.
  • With service providers: We use trusted third-party providers to host data, deliver push notifications, and operate the App. They may process information only on our behalf and under confidentiality obligations.
  • For legal reasons: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Petm, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: If Petm is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Location Information

Location features are optional. The App requests access to your device location only when you enable a feature that needs it, and you can grant or deny this permission through your device settings. You can disable location access at any time in your device’s system settings; some location-based features may then become unavailable. We use location data to provide the requested feature and do not use it for advertising.

5. Your Content and Social Features

Petm is a social platform, so much of what you share is intended to be seen by others. Please be thoughtful about the information, photos, and locations you include in public posts. You can edit or delete your content, and you can delete your account at any time (see Data Retention). Content shared with or saved by other users (for example, a screenshot of a message) may remain outside our control.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. When you delete your account, we delete or de-identify your personal information within a reasonable period, except where we must retain it to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, or enforce our agreements. To request account deletion, use the in-app option or contact us at the email below.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use a strong, unique password and keep it confidential.

8. Your Privacy Rights (California / U.S.)

Petm is intended for users in the United States. Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information. If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) gives you the right to:

  • Know and access the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Delete the personal information we collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Opt out of sale or sharing of personal information — note that we do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Non-discrimination for exercising any of these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at xuruokunabc@gmail.com. We will verify your request and respond as required by law. You may use an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf.

9. Children’s Privacy

Petm is intended for users aged 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice within the App. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
